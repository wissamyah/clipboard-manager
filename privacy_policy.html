Clipboard Manager Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you when you use Clipboard Manager. The extension only accesses and stores clipboard data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data includes text snippets that you copy to your clipboard while the extension is active.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected by Clipboard Manager is used solely for the purpose of providing you with clipboard management functionality. Specifically, the data is used to:

  • Store and display your clipboard history within the extension.
  • Enable search, filtering, and management of clipboard items.
  • Support features like favorites and export functionality.

We do not share, transfer, or sell your clipboard data to any third parties.

Data Security

Security of your data is a priority for us. Clipboard Manager:

  • Ensures that only you can access and manage your clipboard data through the extension interface.
  • Does not store any data on external servers or cloud services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Continued use of the Clipboard Manager Chrome Extension after any modifications to the Privacy Policy constitutes your consent to the updated terms.
